# Demo sample documents — case referral uploads

Real, publicly published documents that are safe and legal to use when demoing the
case referral upload feature. **None of them contain personal data of real
children** — they are official blank forms, government issuances, or training
materials with fictional cases. Verified 2026-07-20 by extracting and reviewing
the text of every file.

> Do NOT use actual case study / case referral reports found on document-sharing
> sites (Scribd, Course Hero, etc.). Many are genuine confidential records about
> real, identifiable minors uploaded without authorization — using them, even in
> a demo, violates the child's privacy and the Data Privacy Act (RA 10173) and
> the confidentiality rules of RA 11642.

| File | What it is | Publisher / source | Why it is legal for demos |
|---|---|---|---|
| `savechildren-inter-agency-referral-form.pdf` | Inter-Agency Referral Form and Guidance Note (2017) — blank referral form + how-to guide | IASC Reference Group on MHPSS, hosted by [Save the Children Resource Centre](https://resourcecentre.savethechildren.net/document/inter-agency-referral-form-and-guidance-note) | Openly published UN inter-agency tool, expressly intended for use in referrals, trainings, and workshops; blank template |
| `gwent-multi-agency-referral-form.docx` | Child Safeguarding Multi-Agency Referral Form (MARF) — blank | [Gwent Safeguarding Board (Wales, UK)](https://www.gwentsafeguarding.org.uk/) | Official public-sector blank form distributed for anyone making a child referral |
| `pa-child-welfare-case-examples.pdf` | Filled-in child welfare case narratives (fictional families, e.g. "the Simpka family") used in caseworker training | [Pennsylvania Child Welfare Resource Center](http://www.pacwrc.pitt.edu/), University of Pittsburgh | Published training handout; all cases are fictional classroom scenarios — good for demoing the AI summary on a document with real narrative text |
| `dswd-mc13-2020-domestic-adoption-guidelines.pdf` | DSWD Memorandum Circular 13 s. 2020 — Omnibus Guidelines on Domestic Adoption (references case study reports and referral flows) | [DSWD adoption office](https://adoption.dswd.gov.ph/) | Official Philippine government issuance, public document |
| `dswd-ao-2007-006.pdf` | DSWD Administrative Order 2007-006 — management of Crisis Intervention Units incl. handling of referred clients | [DSWD](https://dswd.gov.ph/) | Official Philippine government issuance, public document |
| `unicef-laos-cp-sop-2023.pdf` | Child Protection Case Management Standard Operating Procedures (2023) | [UNICEF Laos](https://www.unicef.org/laos/) | Openly published government/UNICEF SOP |

Suggested demo mapping:

- **Upload Case Referral** → `pa-child-welfare-case-examples.pdf` (has extractable
  narrative text, so the AI summary button works) or the blank
  `savechildren-inter-agency-referral-form.pdf` / `gwent-multi-agency-referral-form.docx`.
- **Upload Report** → any of the PDFs; all pass the .pdf/.doc/.docx validator.
